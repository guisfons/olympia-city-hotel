Attractions

Olympia City Hotel by Dara offers many interesting attractions and facilities that are perfect for leisure and business guests looking for short and extended stays in Phnom Penh.

If you are looking for private events in Phnom Penh, such as birthdays, weddings, business or marketing events, Olympia City Hotel is the perfect place with meeting, business and event areas for all types of guests.

We also offer a fully equipped gym and spa to all our guests, as well as a swimming pool for a breezy and relaxing stay at Olympia City Hotel by Dara
