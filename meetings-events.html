& Events

Olympia City Hotel by Dara offers many perks for business at the core of Cambodia’s capital city’s new hub. Spacious and fully equipped halls, boardrooms, spaces for team building, marketing launches and private events in Phnom Penh. Our guests can also count on a 24/7 operating Business Center, with multilingual staff to assist everyone.

Our team of specialists will gladly assist you and your team in planning business-related and private events, arranging guided tours of Phnom Penh landmarks and organizing tailor-made trips to the beach or the Angkor temples.
